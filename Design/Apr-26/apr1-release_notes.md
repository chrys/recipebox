# RecipeBox — April 2026 Release Notes

## 🎯 Overview

We've redesigned the onboarding experience to let visitors explore and draft recipes **before signing up**. This significantly reduces friction for new users while maintaining full account benefits for registered members.

---

## ✨ What's New

### 1. Guest Recipe Browsing
- **Browse public recipes** without creating an account
- View full recipe details, including ingredients, instructions, prep time, and ratings
- Search and filter by category
- Only public recipes are visible to anonymous users

### 2. Guest Recipe Draft Creation
- Start creating a new recipe as an anonymous user
- Fill in title, description, ingredients, instructions, and more
- Your draft is temporarily stored in your browser session
- A persistent banner reminds you to sign up to save permanently

### 3. Automatic Draft Saving on Signup
- When you create an account, any draft recipe in progress is **automatically saved**
- No need to re-enter your recipe data — we handle it for you
- You'll see a welcome message confirming the autosave
- Your new recipe is immediately available in your personal recipe list

### 4. Simplified Guest Navigation
- Guests see a cleaner sidebar with only **Recipes** link visible
- Calendar and Admin Settings are hidden until you sign up
- Shopping List is not available for guest browsing (view-only in future versions)
- Top navigation still shows prominent **Sign Up** and **Login** buttons

### 5. Guest Shopping List Preview (View-Only)
- Anonymous users can visit the Shopping List page
- They'll see information about how it works
- To use the full feature (scheduling recipes, generating lists), sign up

---

## 🔄 User Journeys

### For New Visitors
1. Land on the app → see **Recipes** link in sidebar
2. Browse public recipes without signing up
3. Click a recipe to see full details
4. Click **+ New Recipe** to start creating
5. Fill in your recipe
6. Click submit → see confirmation that it's drafted
7. Click **Sign Up** in the banner → create account
8. Your recipe automatically appears in your account

### For Existing Users
No changes to your experience! All your privileges remain:
- Full access to personal recipe management
- Calendar scheduling and meal planning
- Shopping list generation from scheduled recipes
- Recipe sharing and visibility controls

---

## 🛡️ What's Protected

- **Private recipes** remain invisible to other users
- Only recipe **owners** can edit or delete their recipes
- Anonymous users cannot create accounts, access calendars, or manage shopping lists
- All data is preserved across the new guest functionality
